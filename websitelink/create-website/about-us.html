ABOUT CREAT


Center for Research Engineering & Advance Technologies

CREAT specializes in embedded electronics products related to connected vehicles, telematics, ADAS, infotainment, EV technologies, controllers and sensors, advance lighting and technologies related to next generation automotive needs. The center works in collaboration with other entities of the MINDA group as well as external technology providers.

Specializing in software, hardware, mechanical and creative design, the team of engineers and designers at CREAT work in the following four product spaces:

Cockpit Electronics and Advance Technologies

Body Exterior and Safety Technologies

Creative Design and Innovation

Product Assurance Lab

ABOUT UNO MINDA


Connecting Ideas to Technologies

Making its mark in the International Grid of Automobile Components manufacturing, UNO MINDA steers ahead as a leading Tier 1 supplier of Proprietary Automotive Solutions to Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs). Incepted way back in 1958 with a meagre start-up capital and now notching up a group turnover beyond US$ 875 million, speaks volumes of the conglomerate that it is today.

For nearly six decades, UNO MINDA has made significant contributions to the automotive industry supply chain with innovative products, designed and engineered for efficiency with an emphasis on enhanced comfort levels and fine tuned response.

UNO MINDA has 52 manufacturing plants in India, Indonesia, Vietnam, Spain, Morocco, Mexico Colombia, design centers in Taiwan, Japan & Spain sales offices in USA, Europe, Vietnam & Spain. It also has a strategic sourcing office in China.

With the human edge of a highly motivated workforce of over 15000 team members the Group is headquartered in Manesar, Haryana, India. We have engineering, research and development centers in Manesar, Pune & Sonepat.

The UNO MINDA strategic alignment ensures the maintenance of leadership in providing the ideal product support for their customers. Drawing on the benefits of this long-term customer support relation, UNO MINDA forges ahead as a Tier-1 supplier. We manufacture 2 Wheeler Switches and Handle Bar Assemblies, Sensors, Actuators, Controllers 4 Wheeler Switches and Heater Control Panels, Cigar Lighters, Automotive Lamps, Automotive Horns, Alternate Fuel Systems , Automotive Batteries, Blow Molding Components, Seat Belts, Alloy Wheels, Wheel Covers, Air Filtration Systems, Canisters, Die Casting Components, SRC, Steering Wheels, Airbags, Fuel Caps, Body Sealing, Brake & Fuel Hoses, Car Infotainment Systems, Air Brakes, Clutch Actuation Systems and Automatic Gear Shifters and Telematics & Connected Car Solutions for OEMs.

Technology and innovation are two key pillars of UNO MINDA philosophy. Our underlying vision is to create a culture that fosters great ideas that can be the basis for planning ingenious products, successfully manufactured to deliver value to our customers.

T S Srikanth

Head (Body Electronics & Advanced Technologies)

 A designer knows he has achieved perfection not when there is nothing left to add, but when there is nothing left to take away.  

Srikanth Sathiyanarayanan is currently working as GM—Engineering & Development for CREAT, UNO MINDA. In his current capacity, he oversees the end-end product development of Body Controls, Lighting Controls and Electric Vehicle related electronics. He is bringing in his 18 years of focussed automotive electronics experience with diversified product knowledge— including Safety Electronics, Under the Hood electronics, Cockpit Electronics etc. His experience include product development, technical sales, strategic & product marketing, project management, people management and product strategies.

At CREAT, UNO Minda, his focus is on bringing in global technologies with regional strategies to support both customers and end-consumers.

Prior to joining UNO MINDA, Srikanth was serving as “Chief Engineer—Instrument Clusters” at Visteon India responsible for managing strategy, business development, product development etc., He was associated with Visteon for about 15 years worked with global leaderships & colleagues. Before Joining Visteon, he has worked with Sundaram Clayton (now WABCO) for 3 years on Anti Lock Braking Systems software. He co-authored and published university text book on “computer system architecture and organization” ISBN: 978-81-265-2284-2 through Wiley publication. He holds masters in “embedded technologies” from College of Engineering, Guindy, Chennai and bachelors in “ electrical & electronics engineering” from PSG College of Technology, Peelamedu, Coimbatore.
