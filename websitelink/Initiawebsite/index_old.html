Translating the brand language into form

The language was developed to fit an entire line-up starting from a SCV (small commercial vehicle) to a HCV (Heavy commercial vehicle). The design incorporates the regulatory norms and creates an iconic visual character. I was designed to create a prominent yet a pleasing appeal.

Redefining everyday mobility

This iconic interpritation of the rickshaw reimagines the concept based on what the rickshaw owner actually requires. It tries to incorporate an additional source of income for the rickshaw driver by incorporating advertising boards into the design creating an iconic silhouette that is bound to shape the urban transport landscape. It has strong modern fascia and rear as well, designed incorporating cost effective components but maintaining the richness in design.

Mahindra XUV rendered as next-gen electric coupe

Luxury as new standard

The concept was to envisage a luxury variant of the 2016 Innova crysta interior (by modifying minimum no. of panels). This has been achieved by visually breaking the volume in layers and playing with the contrast and gloss black finish. The floating center panel housing the HVAC and gear function, fuses with the main dashboard and creates a wraparound feel, further accentuated by the ambient lighting. The dual TFT screen and the HUD adds to the experience through a premium interface.

A face for electric charging in India

The various components that are a part of the electric vehicle ecosystem are designed in conjunction with the vehicle design language to create a harmonious product experience. The design process goes through various ideation stages to accommodate complex electrical systems. The end product is a sleek object with a modern aesthetic that seamlessly incorporates the desired functions and provides an optimum user experience.

Style & function for better experience

Instead of following the traditional structure and visual language of cluster, we took an advantage of reconfigurable TFT screen and designed the instrument cluster which will complement the overall visual appeal of the sport bike. Easy user interactions by using the handlebar controls while riding or when it is stopped was the key factor while designing the interactions.

New tactile way to control

The switch is a study in new interaction archetypes that provide an opportunity to create new and exciting designs at the component level. The usual button based clicking interactions are replaced with gestures both physical and touch based. The joystick offers a tactile gesture base to carry out interactions with vehicle instrument clusters. The resistive touch panel explores marrying the swiping gesture with indicating direction of travel. The concept of the switch explores the design of the component for the next generation of users who come from a touch screen and display heavy interaction paradigms.

Expressing performance

A concept adventure motorcycle designed to evoke the feeling of power in its rider. The wild untamed aesthetic is shaped purposefully and it provokes the rider to explore the uncharted territory. The design is however strongly driven by functional needs of an adventure bike and incorporates thoughtful details such as the integrated fog lamp. The bike is designed to tame the wild and the design language cleary showcases that purpose.

Digital surrealism

The Indian automotive industry is transitioning to larger display screens and is giving an immersive user experience to customers. Influenced by these trends, the idea behind this design was to design a fully reconfigurable instrument cluster which connects with other display screens in the vehicle including the infotainment system and a heads-up-display.

A slick & seamless product experience

The gear selector integrates functional requirements and aesthetics with immense attention to detail. The gloss black panel houses the driving mode indicators that are illuminated to clearly communicate the information to the driver. The premium diamond knurling pattern improves tactility of the switch. These intricate details enhance the experience of the user from the smallest possible touch points.

The future of moving people

The concept is an ideation of the next generation urban commuter. It has a micro-car footprint making it ideal for the urban setting that is progressively becoming congested. The vehicle has a strong personality and an iconic character. The side body provides a canvas for the chic urban user to express themselves. The vehicle takes its environment into consideration and has a dot matrix communication panel integrated in the exterior to communicate vital information as well as provide a personal experience to the user. The concept blends into the environment perfectly, having been designed for a specific context, while simultaneously standing out and making a statement.
